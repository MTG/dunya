Palghat R. Raghu (January 9, 1928 - June 2, 2009) was a famous Carnatic musician and percussionist.

Palghat Raghu was a percussion master known for his distinctive style in playing as well as his effortless finger techniques and technical brilliance. His unique style which involves imaginative use of the Toppi (left part of Mridangam) combines melody with rhythm and lifts the concert performance to high levels.
