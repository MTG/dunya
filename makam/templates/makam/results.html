Welcome to Dunya Makam

Dunya-makam is a web application for the analysis and discovery of Turkish makam music, aimed to showcase various technologies developed within the CompMusic project.

You can navigate the analyzed collection using the search bar and the filters at the top of the page. If you want a quick preview of the application, you can jump to the examples below:
